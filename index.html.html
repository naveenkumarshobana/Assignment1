Instructions for Perfect Cup of Tea

  • STEP - 1: Add 1 cup/200 mL of freshly boiled water to your tea bag (in a mug)
  • STEP - 2: Allow the tea bag to brew for 2 minutes.
  • STEP - 3: Remove the tea bag.
  • STEP - 4: Add 10 mL of milk.
  • STEP - 5: Wait 6 minutes before consumption for the cuppa to reach its optimum temperature of 140 F/60 C.
